About

Where economics meets behaviour

I'm an applied economist with a background that sits at an unusual intersection: rigorous quantitative training and a deep grounding in behavioural science. Numbers tell part of the story — understanding how people and institutions actually behave tells the rest.

Over 8+ years across Saudi Arabia's public and private sectors, I've built econometric models at national scale, led cross-government analytical workstreams, and translated complex data into decisions that reached ministerial and executive leadership — including the Ministry of Economy and Planning, Saudi Tourism Authority, Elm, and Takamol Holding.

My work spans policy analysis, demand and market modelling, commercial analytics, and behavioural strategy. Currently advising GCC private-sector and institutional clients through Basar Advisory, and open to senior roles where economic rigour and behavioural insight drive real decisions.
